This is my cat. She is a black cat, which means she is usually one with the shadows, and doesn't bother to enter the third dimension, or render her form properly. She hides in a box under the stairs and hisses at me when I walk past. Don't worry, cat, I'm not here to disturb your liquid form. I am a computer science and art student at the University of Michigan, which is far inferior to this soft ball of darkness, but here are some things I've made by arranging lines on my laptop in pleasing ways.
